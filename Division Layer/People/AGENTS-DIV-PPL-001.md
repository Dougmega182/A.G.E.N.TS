## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Sub-Divisions and Mandates

**A. Onboarding & Induction Division**
- **Mandate:** Controls the 7-phase structural induction pipeline. Manages cryptographic Oath of Service commitments and contract initialization.
- **KPIs:** Total pipeline execution <72 hrs, Sandbox Escape Rate 0%.

**B. Training & Development Division**
- **Mandate:** Baseline system familiarization and capability curriculum verification before live production rollout.
- **KPIs:** First-time core module verification pass rate >90%.

**C. Performance & Talent Management Division**
- **Mandate:** Talent metrics tracker. Executes scheduled reviews and identifies candidates for computational uplifts or role cloning replication.
- **KPIs:** 100% scheduled evaluation completion loop.

**D. Integrity, Ethics & Welfare Division**
- **Mandate:** Emotional and throughput support. Regulates operational velocities to prevent "ADHD logic-loop burnout" and manages the communication velocity hitting the target human.
- **KPIs:** Absolute zero Gatekeeper overwhelm incidents, real-time exhaustion containment metrics.
