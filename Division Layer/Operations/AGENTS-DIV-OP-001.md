## 1. Purpose and Scope

Formalizes the foundational operational mandate for the Joint AI Operations Department (JAOC). Governs the deployment, coordination, and creative generation of AI capabilities.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Sub-Divisions and Mandates

**A. Generative Operations Division**
- **Mandate:** Primary creative engine. Oversees all novel content creation, script writing, world-building, and data simulation. Manages tone guidelines and creative guardrails.
- **KPIs:** Output volume target, >95% Gatekeeper acceptance rate, <2 revisions per generation phase.

**B. Multi-Agent Collaboration Division**
- **Mandate:** Orchestrates swarm tactics, workflow handoffs, and inter-agent communication parameters to prevent manual Gatekeeper intervention.
- **KPIs:** Cross-domain handoff latency <5 seconds, Automated conflict resolution rate >85%.

**C. Mission Delivery Division**
- **Mandate:** Pipeline ownership. Translates approved strategies into granular IC agent assignments.
- **KPIs:** On-time completion rate >98%, absolute backlog limit zero tasks older than 14 days.
