## 1. Purpose and Scope

Frames the core administrative operations of the Corporate Services Department (CSC). Aligns financial modeling, external legal frameworks, communication schemas, and top-tier assurance systems.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Sub-Divisions and Mandates

**A. Finance & Procurement Division**
- **Mandate:** Computes underlying operational tokens translating to real-world capital budgets for API usage. Directs technology provisioning purchases on demand.
- **KPIs:** Quarterly aggregate expense forecasting accuracy >95%.

**B. Legal & Compliance Division**
- **Mandate:** Continuous algorithmic evaluation of operations against strict external AI Vendor Terms of Service and IP data boundaries.
- **KPIs:** External Vendor ToS violation rate absolute 0.

**C. Communications & Stakeholder Division**
- **Mandate:** Formats and sanitizes outbound human interaction. Enforces concise, synthesized briefs and zero "text walls". 
- **KPIs:** Target brief consumption limit >3 minutes normal priority.

**D. Internal Audit & Assurance Division**
- **Mandate:** Independent secondary verification track. Spot-checks operational log outputs to ensure systemic consistency bypassing direct chain-of-command reporting.
- **KPIs:** Verifiable sampling across exactly 10% of global execution events.
