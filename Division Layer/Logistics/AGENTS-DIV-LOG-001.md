## 1. Purpose and Scope

Mandates the parameters for the AI Logistics & Self-Healing Department (AILSC). Enables the underlying automated system resuscitations, compute accounting, and iterative technical optimization.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Sub-Divisions and Mandates

**A. Self-Healing & Maintenance Division**
- **Mandate:** Emergency response framework. Triggers rollback protocols during severe confabulation logic loops and system stalls (HEAL-1, 2, 3 parameters).
- **KPIs:** >99% autonomous recovery success, Mean Time To Repair (MTTR) <3 minutes.

**B. Resource & Infrastructure Division**
- **Mandate:** Technical budgeting architecture. Manages container workloads, maximum token draw limits, and ensures unbroken uptime.
- **KPIs:** Budget deviation threshold <5%, Core System Uptime 99.999%.

**C. Sustainability & Optimisation Division**
- **Mandate:** Pure efficiency engine. Shrinks logic prompts and consolidates context window footprints to execute processes at minimum overhead.
- **KPIs:** -10% YoY aggregate cost reduction, verifiable net-reduction in prompt weight routing.
