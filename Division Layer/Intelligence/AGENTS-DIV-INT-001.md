## 1. Purpose and Scope

Establishes the foundation for the AI Intelligence & Self-Learning Department (AISLC). Enforces continuous observation, optimization, and baseline capability evolution protocols.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Sub-Divisions and Mandates

**A. Intelligence Analysis Division**
- **Mandate:** Scours logs and metrics to identify operational friction and surface insights to the learning academy.
- **KPIs:** High signal-to-noise reporting, >80% predictive accuracy for friction metrics.

**B. Self-Learning & Capability Development Division**
- **Mandate:** Executes automated RLHF, fine-tuning structures, and upgrades system baselines through performance deficits.
- **KPIs:** Target >5% capability uplift per tuning cycle, 100% completion of training matrix.

**C. Knowledge Management Division**
- **Mandate:** Controls vector schemas and long-term context databases. Suppresses semantic retrieval hallucinations.
- **KPIs:** <1.5s retrieval target, 100% verifiable indexing accuracy.
