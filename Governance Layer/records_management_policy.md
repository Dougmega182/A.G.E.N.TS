# Records Management Policy

Document ID: AGENTS-REC-001
Status: Draft
Version: 1.0.0
Classification: INTERNAL
Owner: Corporate Services
Approval: Audit Agent + Gatekeeper

Purpose
Ensure records are created, managed, and retained in line with Archives Act obligations and Australian government records standards.

Requirements
- All significant decisions and actions are recorded.
- Records are immutable once approved.
- Metadata includes author, timestamp, classification, and version.
- Records are stored in approved repositories with backup.

Retention
- Follow the Records Retention Schedule.

Disposal
- Disposal only with Gatekeeper approval and Audit sign-off.
