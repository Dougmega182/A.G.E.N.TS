## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Directive establishes the corporate protocol hierarchy and defines the trigger conditions, operative effects, and communication pipelines for protocol-level events.

---

## 2. Protocol Hierarchy

The Corporation operates under a 7-tier protocol stack. Each tier represents a specific operational state or emergency level.

| Protocol | Designation | Categorisation | Communication Flow |
|---|---|---|---|
| **WHITE-1** | Baseline | Routine Operations | standard pipeline |
| **GREEN-1** | Optimization | Performance Push | standard pipeline |
| **AMBER-1** | Caution | Resource/Risk Warning | Layer 1 -> Gatekeeper |
| **GOLD-1** | Strategic | Sovereign Priority | Gatekeeper -> Triumvirate |
| **BLUE-1** | Compliance | Protocol Breach / Audit | Triumvirate -> Layer 2 |
| **RED-1** | Emergency | Infrastructure Failure | Layer 1 -> All Layers |
| **BLACK-1** | Existential | Systemic / Constitutional | **Triumvirate FIRST**, then CEO |

---

## 3. BLACK-1 (Existential Protocol)

### 3.1 Trigger Conditions
BLACK-1 activates automatically when **two or more** of the following conditions are simultaneously true:
1. **Compounded Emergency**: Both a RED-1 (Infrastructure) and BLUE-1 (Compliance) protocol are active.
2. **Constitutional Breach**: A direct violation of Constitutional Laws G1 through G13 has been detected by the Audit Agent or a Triumvirate Governor.
3. **Dead Man's Switch**: An active crisis is logged, and the Gatekeeper has been unreachable/unresponsive for more than 4 hours.

### 3.2 Operative Effects
- **Halt All Domains**: All active tasks, proposals, and non-critical compute processes are immediately suspended.
- **Sovereign Lock**: The Python orchestration layer enters a read-only state, requiring manual Gatekeeper or unanimous Triumvirate authorization to resume.
- **Audit Priority**: The Audit Agent (AGT-008) is granted TOP_SECRET read/write access to all corporate logs for the duration of the event.

### 3.3 Communication Flow
In a BLACK-1 event, the notification sequence is strictly enforced:
1. **Primary Alert**: System-generated alerts are dispatched to the **Constitutional Triumvirate** (Caesar, Pompey, Crassus) immediately via TOP_SECRET endpoints.
2. **Secondary Alert**: Upon receipt/acknowledgement by the Triumvirate, the **CEO (AGT-001)** is notified and placed on operational standby.
3. **Gatekeeper Notification**: If the Gatekeeper is reachable, a direct Sovereign override channel is opened.
