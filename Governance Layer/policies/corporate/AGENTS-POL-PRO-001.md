## 1. Purpose and Scope

Aligns the logistical acquisition of high-performance compute and third-party SaaS stacks with rigorous structural thresholds and ethical vendor assessments.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Vendor Selection and Vetting
- **Security Check:** All AI software vendors must successfully pass an explicit `Security & Privacy Audit` evaluating their alignment prior to any internal API payload integration.
- **Ethical Sourcing:** We algorithmically prioritize vendors guaranteeing transparency regarding their AI training data origins and those verifying their environmental footprint (e.g., green-power data centers).

### 2.2 Procurement Thresholds
- **<$2,000:** Allowed for direct rapid purchase execution by the Project Manager without chained review.
- **$2,000 - $10,000:** Activation requires three independent market quotes and secondary Tech Lead approval.
- **>$10,000:** Requires hard Director sign-off, plus a formal legal Contract Review cycle.
