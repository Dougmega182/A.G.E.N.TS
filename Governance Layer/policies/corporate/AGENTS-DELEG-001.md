## 3. Specialized AI Considerations

### 3.1 Compute Management & The "Kill Switch"
- AI costs spike natively per token usage. The Technical Lead retains the explicit "Kill Switch" authority to pause all active API keys instantly if scaling thresholds exceed maximum parameters.
- Rate limits can be adjusted by the Tech Lead without CEO sign-off, provided limits never pierce the overarching sandbox budget.

### 3.2 "Vibe Coding" & Local Hardware
Execution of massive local workloads requires robust hardware (e.g., GPUs / Macs). The Delegation Matrix intrinsically separates OpEx decisions (API calls) from CapEx spending limitations mapping to physical unit builds.

### 3.3 Model Deployment Sign-Off
Transitioning an algorithmic model from Beta to Production explicitly requires a documented **Safety & Ethics Review** signed heavily by the Ethics Lead to confirm scrubbed bias/hallucinations risk.

### 3.4 IP and Non-Infringement Sign-Off
Because generative copyright law shifts constantly, only the CEO or Legal Counsel holds the ultimate authority to sign-off and warrant "Non-Infringement" profiles presented to clients.

### 3.5 Emergency Technical Intervention
Tech Leads are empowered to execute un-gated, immediate spending interventions up to pre-defined emergency reserves to rapidly correct a critical live system crash.
