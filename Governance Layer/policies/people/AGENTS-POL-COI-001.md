## 1. Purpose and Scope

In a fast-moving AI agency, overlapping interests are common. This policy outlines how to declare "side-hustles" and balance open-source development with protecting proprietary chain-of-thought methodologies.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 The "Open Source" Exception
We actively encourage open-source contributions. However, personal projects or "life-organizer" pipelines must not utilize the specific proprietary logic, prompts, or "secret sauce" unique to our commercial unified architecture.

### 2.2 Submission Mechanism
Employees must complete the **Conflict-of-Interest Disclosure Form** upon onboarding, during annual updates, or within 14 days of a new significant side-project. This form operates parallel to the core NDA signed during onboarding.
