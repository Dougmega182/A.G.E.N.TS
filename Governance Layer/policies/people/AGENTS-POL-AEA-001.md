## 1. Purpose and Scope

Binds the corporate execution matrix under the strict structural oversight of the *Fair Work Act 2009*, explicitly calibrating compensation and execution expectations involving AI frameworks.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Award Alignment & Classification
- **BOOT Testing:** Every human Enterprise Agreement or IFA must empirically pass the "Better Off Overall Test" (BOOT) against the relevant Modern Awards.
- **Classification Engine:** Explicit definitions separate internal scaling boundaries:
    - *Junior AI Dev:* Prompt testing / execution evaluation.
    - *Senior Architect:* Workflow mapping / System prompt generation.
    - *Project Lead:* Client-facing strategy and execution sign-off.

### 2.2 Flexibility and Technological Shifts
- **IFAs:** Agreements must include formalized Individual Flexibility Arrangements ensuring asynchronous start/finish times natively supporting "Vibe Coding" architectures.
- **Consultation Mandate:** The agency enforces a strict **2-week mandatory consultation period** prior to executing any major technical framework shift (e.g., migrating foundational LLM models) that directly impacts workplace execution or skill value.
