## 1. Purpose and Scope

This standard aligns the corporation with the *Commonwealth Supplier Code of Conduct (2024)*, ensuring absolute compliance for governmental contracting.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Integrity
We explicitly comply with the Commonwealth Supplier Code of Conduct (2024). Operational matrices must never be deployed if they compromise the security of governmental infrastructure.

### 2.2 Accountability
The Corporation will maintain highly accurate immutable logs (`checkpoints.jsonl`) of all government project work and allow for external independent audits automatically upon demand.

### 2.3 Conflict of Interest
All operational agents and human directors must immediately disclose any perceived or actual conflicts through the `AGENTS-POL-COI-001` framework before bidding for any government tenders.

---

## 3. Records Management and Retention
Government audit records are classified RESTRICTED and maintained persistently pursuant to `AGENTS-RET-001`.
