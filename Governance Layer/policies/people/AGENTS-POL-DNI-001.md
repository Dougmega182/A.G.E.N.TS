## 1. Purpose and Scope

Mandates inclusive operation both via human resourcing recruitment channels and across the systemic deployment of non-biased execution agents.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Blinded Recruitment
Where technically viable, performance assessments for human AI Engineering roles are functionally "blinded" up until the finalized stage to permanently sever unconscious bias regarding gender, age, orientation or ethnicity.

### 2.2 Inclusive AI Design
Algorithm-driven project generations, document evaluations, and prompt arrays must be stress-tested explicitly for demographic bias to verify the AI outputs do not natively disadvantage specific corporate or public subsets. 

### 2.3 Neurodiversity Support
The A.G.E.N.T.S. Corporation expressly recognizes neurodiversity as a strategic technical advantage. Environment configurations provide asynchronous flexibility and implement explicit **"Low-Sensory Focus Blocks"** to support variable cognitive execution styles during deep engineering cycles.
