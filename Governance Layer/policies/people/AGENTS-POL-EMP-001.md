## 1. Purpose and Scope

A central repository guiding the operational interaction parameters between human personnel and our global agentic swarms. It sets boundaries between asynchronous freedom and core strategic alignment.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Ways of Working & "Vibe Coding"
- **Vibe Coding Parameters:** Human developers have vast flexibility to code and guide agent swarms asynchronously throughout the week.
- **Core Hours:** To ensure critical project syncing, all human and agent overlap must strictly adhere to the **10 AM – 3 PM** 'Core Hours' envelope. 
- **Communications:** Formal escalation happens via Slack arrays following established protocols to prevent agent execution looping. 

### 2.2 IT & Security
- **Data Hardening:** Corporate laptops require absolute encryption. Access to live inference pipelines mandates mandatory Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- **Local Model Usage:** Work executed via local LLM stacks on corporate hardware must remain siloed from external private drives.

### 2.3 Leave & Innovation
- Personnel are entitled to standard sick/parental leaves under Fair Work mandates.
- **Innovation Days:** We provide scheduled, independent R&D time ("Innovation Days") aimed strictly at personal tech growth and experimenting with new swarm architectures disconnected from the immediate commercial pipeline.
