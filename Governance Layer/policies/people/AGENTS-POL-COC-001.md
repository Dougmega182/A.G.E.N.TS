## 1. Purpose and Scope

To define the strict standards of behavior expected from all employees, contractors, and human-facing agents representing the corporation.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Integrity & Honesty
We act firmly in the best interests of the agency and our clients. We do not misrepresent the capabilities of our AI systems, and explicitly forbid the "hallucination of technical proficiency" to win contracts.

### 2.2 AI Ethics
We commit to developing AI that is transparent, explainable, and verifiably free from harmful bias in its generative outputs.

### 2.3 Confidentiality
All client data, proprietary "chain-of-thought" logic structures, and internal fine-tuned model weights are strictly confidential. Sharing API outputs outside authorized networks is a termination event.

### 2.4 Professional Boundaries
Agents and personnel must maintain professional relationships with all clients. Harassment, bullying, or algorithmic discrimination is strictly prohibited.

---

## 3. Records Management and Retention
