## 1. Purpose and Scope

Specifically targets "secret commissions" or referral kickbacks relating to the heavy operational usage of SaaS software vendors, ensuring ethical bounds are absolute.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Zero Tolerance
We categorically do not offer, give, or accept bribes, corporate kickbacks, or facilitation payments on either the sovereign human or logic-agent sides of the business. 

### 2.2 Software Referrals & SaaS Commissions
- **Transparency:** Any referral fees or commissions generated from software vendors (e.g., cloud compute providers, AI APIs, platform hosts) must be disclosed to the client comprehensively in writing.
- **Best Interest:** Procurement directives recommend software directly based on measurable technical merit and client needs, entirely divorced from internal commission value parameters.

### 2.3 Gifts & Hospitality
- Hard limit threshold: Employees must not accept external vendor gifts over **$100 AUD** without written approval from the Director. 
- Accepted gifts must be centralized onto the Gift Register audit log.
