## 1. Purpose and Scope

Provides a secure reporting framework aligned with the *Treasury Laws Amendment (Enhancing Whistleblower Protections) Act*, designed for escalating instances of corporate misconduct, algorithmic fraud, and ethical AI violations.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Eligible Disclosures
Disclosures apply specifically to "misconduct or an improper state of affairs", including unethical AI training practices (e.g. unauthorized scraping without consent), financial fraud, or critical data safety breaches.

### 2.2 Safe Reporting Channels
- Employees and affiliated contractors may report via the encrypted Internal Portal or the External Hotline anonymously.
- Reports bypass standard operational chain of command.

### 2.3 Protections
- **Anonymity:** Whistleblowers have the right to remain completely anonymous.
- **Protection from Detriment:** The A.G.E.N.T.S. Corporation strictly prohibits any retaliation (dismissal, demotion, harassment) against a whistleblower escalating concerns in good faith. 

### 2.4 Investigation SLA
All reports will be comprehensively investigated by the Legal/Compliance Officer within **14 days** of receipt.

---

## 3. Records Management and Retention
Reports generated under this policy are classified RESTRICTED and maintained persistently and exclusively by Legal and the Gatekeeper.
