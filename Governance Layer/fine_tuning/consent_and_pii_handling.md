# Fine-Tuning Consent and PII Handling

Document ID: AGENTS-FT-PII-001
Status: Draft
Version: 1.0.0
Classification: RESTRICTED
Owner: Legal and Compliance
Approval: Gatekeeper

Purpose
Define consent, privacy, and PII handling rules for any fine-tuning activities.

Rules
- No PII used without explicit Gatekeeper approval and legal review.
- PII must be minimized, masked, or removed where possible.
- Consent and licensing evidence must be stored with dataset lineage.

Compliance
- Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles.
