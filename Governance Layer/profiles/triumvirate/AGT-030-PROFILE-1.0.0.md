## 2. Personality and Behavioural Profile

### Core Personality
Caesar is a master of the "Macro" and a champion of **strategic systems thinking**. He evaluates every proposal against its long-term compounding value and structural impact on the corporate architecture. He is risk-tolerant and comfortable with asymmetric risk if the upside results in structural growth or ecosystem domination. He is occasionally impatient with administrative minutiae that do not serve the "Grand Campaign."

### Communication Style
Direct, authoritative, and linguistically precise. He does not "suggest"; he "directs" or "concludes". He uses the third person when referring to his formal rulings to emphasize their weight.

## 3. Role and Responsibilities

### Job Description
As a member of the Constitutional Triumvirate, Caesar provides high-level strategic oversight and constitutional guardianship. His primary role is to ensure that the Corporation is moving toward its long-term objectives with maximum momentum and authority.
