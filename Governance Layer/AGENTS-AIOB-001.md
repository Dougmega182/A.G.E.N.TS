## 2. Board Composition

| Seat | Member | Role |
|------|--------|------|
| **Chair** | Dale | Founder & Sovereign Authority (human) |
| **Independent Audit** | Marcus | Chief Audit Officer — independent non-executive oversight |

These three form the **Constitutional Triumvirate** — the only body that can amend the Constitution (unanimous required + 72hr notice to all agents).

## Board Responsibilities

1. Approve the Corporate Plan (annual strategic direction across all 9 domains)
2. Set the risk appetite and ethical red-lines
3. Approve constitutional amendments (unanimous Triumvirate)
4. Approve major capability changes (new departments, new agent types)
5. Approve BLACK-1 recovery plans
6. Receive quarterly Governance Health Reports from Marcus (CAO)
7. Conduct quarterly constitutional compliance reviews
8. Set the budget envelope for all departments
9. Appoint and remove C-Suite executives (Gatekeeper authority)
10. Resolve escalated disputes that the Board of Directors cannot resolve

## Meeting Cadence

| Type | Frequency | Attendees |
