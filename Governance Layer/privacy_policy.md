# Privacy Policy

Document ID: AGENTS-PRIV-001
Status: Draft
Version: 1.0.0
Classification: RESTRICTED
Owner: Legal and Compliance
Approval: Gatekeeper

Purpose
Ensure compliance with the Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles.

Principles
- Only collect information required for mission delivery.
- Use and disclose only for authorised purposes.
- Secure storage and controlled access.
- Provide access and correction pathways.

Collection
- Collect only the minimum data required to execute approved tasks.
- Health and family data are restricted to Gatekeeper and authorised roles.

Use and Disclosure
- Follow Information Sharing Rules and classification levels.
- No external disclosure without Gatekeeper approval.

Security
- Apply ISM and Essential Eight controls for confidentiality and integrity.

Access and Correction
- Gatekeeper can access and correct personal data on request.
- Requests are logged and retained.

Data Breach Response
- Follow incident protocols and legal notification requirements.
