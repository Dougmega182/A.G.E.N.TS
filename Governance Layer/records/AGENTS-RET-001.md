## 1. Purpose and Scope

Define minimum retention periods for corporate records within the ecosystem in strict line with Archives Act obligations.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### Schedule
| Record Type | Retention Period | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Audit logs | 7 years minimum | Immutable, append-only |
| Decision logs | 7 years minimum | Includes approvals and rejections |
| Oath records | Life of agent + 7 years | Retain after decommissioning |
| Employment contracts | Life of agent + 7 years | Retain after termination |
| Incident reports | 7 years minimum | Includes RED and BLUE events |
| Security access logs | 2 years minimum | PSPF and ISM aligned |
| Training records | 7 years minimum | Required for compliance |
| Financial records | 7 years minimum | Tax and audit alignment |
| Privacy requests | 7 years minimum | APP compliance |

### Disposal
- Disposal only with Gatekeeper approval and Audit sign-off.
- Disposal actions must be securely logged.
