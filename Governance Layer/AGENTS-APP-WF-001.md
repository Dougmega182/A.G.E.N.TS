## 1. Purpose
Define the approval lifecycle for all policies, SOPs, contracts, and governance artifacts to meet Australian government standards for accountability and recordkeeping.

Workflow Stages
1. Draft
2. Internal Review (owner + Legal and Compliance)
3. Audit Review (Audit Agent)
4. Governance Review (Constitutional Triumvirate)
5. Gatekeeper Approval
6. Approved and Published

Minimum Approval Requirements
- Governance and constitutional documents require Triumvirate + Gatekeeper.
- Security and privacy documents require Audit Agent + Gatekeeper.
- HR and contract documents require People Command + Legal + Gatekeeper.

Approval Records
- Every approval has a signed record with timestamp, approver, version, and decision.
- Records are stored in Governance Layer/records/approvals/.

Change Control
- Every change must include rationale, risk impact, and backward compatibility note.
- Major changes require re-approval through the full workflow.

Publication
- Approved documents are immutable. Any update creates a new version and leaves prior versions retained.
