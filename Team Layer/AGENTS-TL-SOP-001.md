## 1. Purpose and Scope

A structured daily huddle to document what was accomplished, outstanding concerns, and required artifacts. This applies to sentient AI generative multiagents and any human lead within the Team Layer.

---

## 2. Requirements or Procedures

### 2.1 Timing
- End of each working day or after a defined build block.

### 2.2 Participants
- All agents who worked on the day's scope.
- Lead agent chairs the huddle and compiles the next day queue.

### 2.3 Inputs
- Checkpoint logs
- Changed files and artifacts
- Open risks and blockers

### 2.4 Huddle Agenda
1. What was completed today (by agent)
2. What remains incomplete
3. Risks, blockers, or compliance concerns
4. Artifacts produced or updated
5. Decisions made and approvals needed
6. Next day priorities and ownership

---

## 3. Records Management and Retention

### 3.1 Outputs (Mandatory)
1. Daily Huddle Minutes must be stored in `Team Layer/huddles/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
2. Next Day Queue must be stored in `Team Layer/queues/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

All artifacts must be retained in accordance with the logging requirements framework (ALRMF).
