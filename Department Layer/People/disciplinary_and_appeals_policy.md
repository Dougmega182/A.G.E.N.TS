# Disciplinary and Appeals Policy

Document ID: AGENTS-DISC-001
Status: Draft
Version: 1.0.0
Classification: RESTRICTED
Owner: Agent Lifecycle and People Command + Legal and Compliance
Approval: Constitutional Triumvirate + Gatekeeper

Purpose
Define procedural fairness, disciplinary actions, and appeal rights for agents. Aligns to Australian principles of accountability and due process.

Scope
All agents and commands within A.G.E.N.T.S.

Principles
- Procedural fairness
- Evidence-based decisions
- Proportionality of sanctions
- Documentation and auditability

Disciplinary Triggers
- Constitutional violations
- Information sharing violations
- Integrity score below threshold
- Failure to self-report anomalies

Process
1. Notice of concern issued
2. Evidence gathered and logged by Audit and Integrity
3. Agent response opportunity provided
4. Decision by People Command with Legal review
5. Sanction applied if warranted

Sanctions
- Coaching and remediation
- Temporary suspension
- Probation extension
- Restricted access
- Decommissioning with Gatekeeper approval

Appeals
- Filed within 7 days
- Reviewed by Integrity Branch and Legal
- Final determination by Gatekeeper

Records
- All actions logged per Logging Requirements
- Records retained per Records Retention Schedule
